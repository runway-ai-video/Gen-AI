Privacy Policy

Protecting your subconscious data and AI dream synthesis privacy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Introduction

At Gen AI - video, we understand that your subconscious design processes and AI dream synthesis data are deeply personal. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you engage with our intuitive creation platform and visualized memory services.

Data We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communication history and support interactions

Creative Data

  • AI-generated video content and dream synthesis outputs
  • Input prompts and subconscious design parameters
  • Usage patterns and intuitive creation preferences
  • Runway AI and Runway ML integration data

Technical Information

  • Device information and browser specifications
  • IP address and location data
  • Session logs and performance metrics
  • Error reports and diagnostic information

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience with our AI dream synthesis platform:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us optimize AI processing and visualized memory rendering
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your subconscious design settings and creative preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand usage patterns to improve our intuitive creation tools

How We Use Your Data

  • Process and generate AI video content through Runway AI integration
  • Improve our subconscious design algorithms and dream synthesis capabilities
  • Provide personalized recommendations for intuitive creation workflows
  • Maintain platform security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Communicate updates about new features and visualized memory enhancements
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize AI processing performance
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with trusted partners who help us deliver our AI dream synthesis services:

  • Runway ML Partners: For AI video processing and generation services
  • Cloud Storage Providers: To securely store your visualized memory content
  • Analytics Services: To understand platform usage and improve user experience
  • Payment Processors: To handle billing and management
  • Legal Requirements: When required by or to protect our rights

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data and AI-generated content
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data and subconscious design history
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke permission for data processing at any time

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your subconscious design data and AI-generated content. This includes encryption in transit and at rest, secure access controls, regular security audits, and compliance with data protection standards. Your visualized memory content and intuitive creation data are stored in secure, geographically distributed data centers with multiple layers of protection.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data and AI dream synthesis content only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Account data is typically retained for the duration of your plus 30 days. AI-generated content and visualized memory data may be retained longer for service improvement purposes, unless you specifically request deletion.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your residence, including the United States where our Runway AI integration services are hosted. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions to protect your subconscious design data during international transfers.
